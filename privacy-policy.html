Privacy Policy for Wo zum Teufel

Last updated: July 2025

Overview

Wo zum Teufel is a location guessing game that allows users to participate in photo challenges and location guessing.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Library Access

  • We request camera and photo library permissions to allow users to take and upload photos for participation in the game
  • Photos are uploaded to our servers and may be displayed to other users as part of the game
  • We do not store photos permanently on your device beyond what's necessary for the app functionality

Device Information

  • Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting
  • No personally identifiable information is collected beyond what you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core game functionality
  • To display photos and locations in the game
  • To calculate scores and rankings
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • For marketing and promotional purposes (with your consent or as permitted by law)

Data Sharing

  • Photos may be shared with other users as part of the game
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Data may be shared with service providers who assist in app operation

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • Photos and data are transmitted securely

Your Rights

  • You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings
  • You can delete your account and associated data by contacting us

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: info@code-schmiede.ch

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
